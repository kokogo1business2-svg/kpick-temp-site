Cited in Peer-Reviewed Clinical Research — 2025

Minimally invasive transanal excision for rectal tumors: technical feasibility compared to conventional approach

EROP’s New Port single-port trocar (KSILS-2PB) was used by surgeons at St. Vincent’s Hospital, Catholic University of Korea, in a clinical study of 133 patients comparing minimally invasive and conventional transanal surgical techniques. The study was published in the Annals of Coloproctology, Vol. 41(2), pp. 162–168 (April 2025). Authors: Mun JY, Geong GS, Yoo N, Kim HJ, Cho HM, Kye BH.
